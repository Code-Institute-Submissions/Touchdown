How to Play
  1. Select your preferred difficulty. (Rookie is the default setting)
  2. Click on Start Game. The cards labelled RUN, RUSH, KICK and PASS will flash in a random order.
  3. For each round, 1 card will flash. You MUST remember the order in which they flash.
  4. When the sequence has finished you MUST select the card in the order they appeared.
  5. If you are correct, you will gain 3 points per card and progress to the next round. If you are incorrect you will lose 1 life, 5 points and start from round 1.
  6. If you make it to the 10th round you will complete Touchdown and finish the game. YOUR TOTAL POINTS will be calculated by POINTS GAINED X LIVES LEFT. If you lose all 3 lives it will be GAME OVER.
  7. GOOD LUCK AND HAVE FUN :)
