How to Play
  1. Click on Start Game
  2. The cards labelled RUN, RUSH, KICK and PASS will flash in a random order
  3. For each round 1 card will flash. You MUST remember the order in which they flash.
  4. When the sequence has finished you MUST select the cards in the order they appeared.
  5. If you are correct, you will progress to the next round. If you are incorrect you will lose 1 life.
  6. If you make it to the 10th round you will gain a Touchdown and complete the game. If you lose all 3 lives it will be GAME OVER.
  7. GOOD LUCK AND HAVE FUN :)
